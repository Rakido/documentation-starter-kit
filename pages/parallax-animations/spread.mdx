# Éclatement d'images

import NxParallaxSpread from '../../components/NxParallaxSpread'

## Structure

Il est possible d'épingler des éléments et de les animer au scroll.

La propriété `data-parallax-pin` permet de spécifier l'angle de l'élément à épingler.

```html
<div class="parallax-wrapper">
    <!-- Center image -->
    <img 
        src="https://picsum.photos/200/200?4" 
        data-parallax 
        data-parallax-direction="xy" 
        data-speed="0.3"
        data-scrub="1"
        class="parallax-element"
        alt="Center Image"
    >
    <!-- Top Left -->
    <img 
        src="https://picsum.photos/200/200?5" 
        data-parallax 
        data-parallax-pin="top-left"
        data-speed="0.9"
        data-scrub="0.6"
        class="parallax-element"
        alt="Top Left Image"
    >
    <!-- Top Right -->
    <img 
        src="https://picsum.photos/200/200?6" 
        data-parallax 
        data-parallax-pin="top-right"
        data-speed="0.6"
        data-scrub="1.2"
        class="parallax-element"
        alt="Top Right Image"
    >
    <!-- Bottom Left -->
    <img 
        src="https://picsum.photos/200/200?7" 
        data-parallax 
        data-parallax-pin="bottom-left"
        data-speed="0.8"
        data-scrub="1.2"
        class="parallax-element"
        alt="Bottom Left Image"
    >
    <!-- Bottom Right -->
    <img 
        src="https://picsum.photos/200/200?8" 
        data-parallax 
        data-parallax-pin="bottom-right"
        data-speed="0.5"
        data-scrub="0.7"
        class="parallax-element"
        alt="Bottom Right Image"
    >
</div>
<div class="description">
    Corner Pin Parallax
</div>
```
